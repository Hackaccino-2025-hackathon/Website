    <p class="mb-4">Decentralized applications (dApps) are a revolutionary type of software application that leverages the power of blockchain technology. Unlike traditional applications that rely on centralized servers, dApps operate on a distributed network of computers, offering enhanced security, transparency, and user control.</p>

    <h1 class="text-3xl font-bold mb-4">Understanding dApps</h1>

    <p class="mb-4">Traditional applications rely on central servers controlled by a single entity. This centralization creates vulnerabilities, including single points of failure, censorship, and data manipulation. DApps, on the other hand, distribute their operations across a blockchain network. This decentralized architecture eliminates single points of failure, making dApps more resilient and resistant to censorship and manipulation.</p>

    <h1 class="text-3xl font-bold mb-4">Key Characteristics of dApps</h1>

        <li><b>Decentralized:</b>  Operate on a distributed network, eliminating single points of failure and control.</li>
        <li><b>Cryptographically Secure:</b> Use cryptography to ensure data integrity and security.</li>
        <li><b>Incentivized:</b> Often use tokens to incentivize users and developers.</li>
    </ul>
